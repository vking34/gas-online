ICT GAS JOINTSTOCK CORPORATION

ICT Gas Jointstock Corporation is an industry group in Vietnam. Besides working in petroleum and natural gas, the company has significant subsidiaries active in the fields of insurance, transport and trading. We are Vietnam's leading oil and gas producing company. The main offices are located in Ha Noi.

History

The forerunner of ICT Gas Jointstock Company was the Oil, lub and Grease Corporation which was set up by 4 millionaires: Le Van Vuong, Ha Quy Dung, Ngo Anh Tuan and Abiyyu Fahmi Fajri on 11 January of 1956, and was reestablished by Decision 224/TTG of 17 April 1997 by the Prime Minister.

55 years of building and development of ICT Gas Jointstock Company was closely linked with historic events in the fight to defend and construct the country.
